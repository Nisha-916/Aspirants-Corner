What is SSB?

The Services Selection Board (SSB) interview is a critical part of the selection process for entry into the Indian Armed Forces. This page is dedicated to providing aspirants with all the necessary information to crack the SSB interview successfully.

SSB Stages

  • Stage 1: Screening Test (OIR Test + Picture Perception and Discussion Test)
  • Stage 2: Psychological Tests (TAT, WAT, SRT, SD)
  • Stage 3: Group Testing Officer (GTO) Tasks
  • Stage 4: Interview
  • Stage 5: Conference

Preparation Tips

Here are some key tips to help you prepare for the SSB interview:

  • Enhance your communication skills and confidence.
  • Stay updated on current affairs and defense-related topics.
  • Practice psychological tests like TAT, WAT, SRT, and SD.
  • Participate in group discussions and leadership exercises.
